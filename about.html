About us!

Cooking is an activity the vast majority of people do every day. People need food to stay alive and healthy so cooking is a very important skill to have. Unfortunately, many people do not have time to cook, go grocery shopping, or even plan and prepare for meals. This web application's main purpose is to provide various meal plans suiting a wide variety of diets/lifestyles and delivering those ingredients fresh from the farm and to your doorstep. This software project provides a simple and easy way for users to choose their meals as well as their ingredients all from the comfort of their homes at the click of a button. This will be very useful to any individual who may be too busy in their day-to-day lives but still want the warmth of a home cooked meal while also helping them embrace their inner chef.







Our Goals

• Give people access to fresh ingredients

• Provide healthy meal plans for various diets

• Reduce food waste by sending pre-portioned ingredients

• Expose people to more recipes and cooking techniques

• Provide quick and organised home-cooking
